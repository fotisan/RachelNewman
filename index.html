A safe space to explore and grow

Relationships are the cornerstone of our well-being. Whether you are dealing with family conflict, the pain of grief, or the stress of life transitions, you don't have to do it alone.

My approach is rooted in Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) and Attachment Theory. I work collaboratively with adults and elders (65+) to uncover the root causes of distress, rather than just treating the symptoms.

I understand that reaching out for help takes courage. My practice is designed to be a non-judgmental environment where we can safely explore your history, your patterns, and your hopes for the future.
